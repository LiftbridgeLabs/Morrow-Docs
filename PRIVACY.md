# Privacy

Morrow is designed to connect directly to audiobook servers chosen and operated by the user.

A complete production privacy policy will be published before public release. Until then, this page records the intended privacy principles:

- Morrow does not provide or host audiobook content.
- Server credentials are used only to connect to the server configured by the user.
- Sensitive credentials should be stored using platform-appropriate secure storage.
- Morrow should not sell personal information or library data.
- Any diagnostics, analytics, crash reporting, or third-party services included in a public build will be disclosed here before release.

This document is informational during development and is not yet the final App Store privacy policy.

For privacy questions, open a support issue without including passwords, tokens, private URLs, or other sensitive information.
